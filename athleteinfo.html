Athletes have been pushing the limits of DMT for about 50 years and they have no intention of stopping. Some of these athletes even have entire passes named after them such as, "The Padilla", named after DMT legend Ruben Padilla. But let's get into the athletes.

Here are the top 3 athletes from the 2023 DMT world cup in Palm Beach Florida. (Check the athletes off once you know about them!)

  • Ruben Padilla is 22 years old, he currently trains at Wasatch T&T in Draper, Utah, and is on the USA gymnastics team for Double mini. He has won 4 Double Mini Trampoline world championships.
  • Thomas Minc is 18 years old, he currently trains at Dynamite Gymnastics in Rockville Maryland.This year was his first World cup win.
  • Nolan Zurek is 20 years old, he trains at Phoenix Gymnastics Centre in Edmonton, Canada. This was his first world cup, but he has competed at WAGs (World age groups) multiple times.
