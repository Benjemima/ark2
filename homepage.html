The double mini trampoline was invented in 1970 when two men, Robert F. Bollinger and George Nissen, put two mini trampolines together. The first one was at an angle and the second one was flat. You use the first one to gain height and the second trampoline to dismount. In 1973 the first Double Mini Trampoline World Age Group competition in Tulsa, Oklohoma happened, and that was the start of the years to come. (Press Space to change the background)
